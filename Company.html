Innovation & NeuroTechnology

Revolutionizing Brainwave Insights

At Sona, innovation is at the core of everything we do. Our dedicated R&D team combines neuroscientific research with advanced machine learning to decode your brainwaves in real time. This breakthrough technology empowers you with actionable insights to maximize focus and balance your emotions.

Backed by strategic investments in neurotechnology research, our projects are supported by renowned institutions such as the Neural Interfaces Initiative @ MIT Media Lab, Cognitive Design Fund, Harvard Human Perception Collective, and the Providence Interdisciplinary Futures Lab.
