Key Features

NeuroFocus™ Focus Enhancement

Sona Brainwave X1 continuously monitors your brain’s alpha and beta waves. When cognitive fatigue is detected, our NeuroFocus™ system gently delivers 40Hz rhythmic auditory stimulation alongside subtle haptic feedback, helping you regain a state of deep concentration.

NeuroCalm™ Emotion & Stress Relief

Utilizing advanced EEG signal analysis and machine learning, Sona detects emotional shifts and stress. It then engages NeuroCalm™ mode by playing soothing sounds and triggering gentle vibrations to help you restore balance and relax.

Neuro-Adaptive Wake-Up & Sleep

By continuously analyzing your sleep cycles, Sona Brainwave X1 gently wakes you during a light sleep phase for a refreshed start. It also helps you fall asleep by providing personalized, calming auditory cues tailored to your neural rhythms.

Seamless App Integration

Effortlessly connect Sona Brainwave X1 with your smartphone and companion app. Access real-time neurofeedback, personalized insights, and firmware updates over Bluetooth for a truly connected experience.
