NeuroFocus™ Focus Enhancement

Sona Brainwave X1 continuously monitors your brain’s alpha and beta waves in real time. When it detects cognitive fatigue, it triggers our NeuroFocus™ system with 40Hz rhythmic audio pulses and gentle haptic feedback to re-engage your prefrontal cortex, restoring deep focus.

NeuroCalm™: Emotion Recognition & Relaxation

Using advanced EEG signal analysis, Sona detects shifts in your emotional state. When stress or anxiety arises, it activates NeuroCalm™ – playing soothing sounds like pink noise and delivering subtle vibrations to restore balance and calm your mind.

Neuro-Adaptive Wake-Up

Leveraging EEG-based sleep stage detection, Sona gently wakes you at the optimal moment. By monitoring your sleep cycles, it ensures you arise during a light sleep phase for a naturally refreshed start—seamlessly integrating with smart devices for a personalized morning routine.

Assist Sleep & Recovery

Struggling to fall asleep? Sona supports your nightly rest by playing tailored auditory cues and providing mild stimulation, guiding you into a state of relaxation. Continuous monitoring ensures dynamic adjustments so you enjoy rejuvenating, high-quality sleep each night.

About Sona

Pioneering NeuroTech Innovation Since 2024

Founded by a team of innovative students from Brown University, RISD, MIT, and Harvard, Sona is redefining the way we interact with our minds. We fuse neuroscience, speculative design, and AI-driven analytics to transform brainwaves into actionable insights that help you master focus, regulate emotions, and optimize sleep.

Every Sona product is meticulously engineered with state-of-the-art dry-electrode EEG sensors and adaptive machine learning algorithms, ensuring comfort, precision, and personalized interventions for a better daily experience.
